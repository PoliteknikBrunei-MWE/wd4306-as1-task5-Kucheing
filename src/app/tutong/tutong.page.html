Tutong Tutong District Tutong District (Malay: Daerah Tutong; Jawi: دائيره توتوڠ‎) or simply Tutong is one of the four districts of Brunei. It has an area of 1,166 square kilometres (450 sq mi); the population was 48,313 in 2016. The administrative town is Pekan Tutong. It is home to Tasek Merimbun, the country's largest natural lake.
